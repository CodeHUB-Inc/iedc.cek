About Us

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) is an active organization aiming to foster innovation culture in our institution. IEDC provides venues for creative students to learn and collaborate their innovative ideas into prototypes of viable products and services. We promote technology-based startup ventures from the college students and provide them with access to requirements. We organize various workshops, webinars, talks, and competitions for developing innovative ideas to start new ventures as entrepreneurs.
